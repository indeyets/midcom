You can use the boolean operators AND, OR and NOT (must be capitalized).<br/>
Check the <a href="http://search.cpan.org/dist/Plucene/lib/Plucene.pm">Plucene</a> and <a href="http://jakarta.apache.org/lucene/docs/queryparsersyntax.html">Lucene Documentation</a> for further details.

Katso <a href="http://search.cpan.org/dist/Plucene/lib/Plucene.pm">Plucene</a> ja <a href="http://jakarta.apache.org/lucene/docs/queryparsersyntax.html">Lucene Documentation</a>, jos haluat lisätietoja.
