net.nehmer.blog style elements
==============================

## Article display index

<(index-start)> begins the article index, <(index-item)> displays an individual article and <(index-end)> ends the index. If there are no articles, <(index-empty)> is shown between <(index-start)> and <(index-end)> instead.

## Individual article display

The <(view)> element is used for displaying individual news items.

## Archive of old articles

The archive welcome page begins with the <(archive-welcome-start)> element. Each year is shown with the <(archive-welcome-year)> element. The list ends with <(archive-welcome-end)>.

The month archives begin with <(archive-list-start)>, <(archive-list-item)> displays an individual article and <(archive-list-end)> ends the index. If there are no articles, <(archive-list-empty)> is shown between <(archive-list-start)> and <(archive-list-end)> instead.

## Feeds listing page

<(feeds)> element lists the available news feeds in human-readable format.
